Staten Island: Hidden Parks, Big Scenery

Just a short ferry ride away from the hustle of Manhattan, you’ll find a completely different side of New York. Staten Island reveals expansive coastlines, peaceful trails, and lush natural scenery—offering a rare sense of calm within the city. Here, you’ll discover a place that invites you to slow down and rediscover the quieter beauty of New York.

Staten Island Zoo

The Staten Island Zoo is known for its impressive reptile collection and family-friendly environment, making it a relaxing destination for a half-day visit.

Historic Richmond Town

Historic Richmond Town is a living history village that preserves 18th-century buildings and offers immersive experiences of early colonial life in New York.

Staten Island Museum

This multidisciplinary museum features natural history, art collections, and cultural exhibits, giving visitors a broad look at Staten Island’s past and present.
